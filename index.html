FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all. Most of our clients give us a key, code, or lockbox access. We're fully insured and background-checked for your peace of mind.

How soon can you come?

We often have same-week openings. For deep cleans and move-outs, we recommend booking 3–5 days ahead.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes — all cleaning products and equipment are included. Prefer eco-friendly or fragrance-free products? Just ask.

How is pricing calculated?

Based on the size of your home, the type of clean, and how often you'd like us to visit. As a reference, a 4-bedroom / 4-bathroom home typically runs $130–$140 weekly, $145–$160 bi-weekly, or $180–$200 monthly. Deep cleans start at $650 and post-construction cleans at $500. Every quote is flat and upfront — no surprises.

Are you insured?

Absolutely. We're fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of Florida.

What if I'm not happy with the cleaning?

Let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back and re-clean any area — free of charge. Your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed.
